You’re offline or the server can’t be reached

The coordinator portal needs a connection to your NORSU OJT DTR server (for example this computer’s XAMPP or your school network). Turning off the internet still works if you open the app via localhost or your LAN address while the server is running.

  • Check Wi‑Fi / data, or use the same Wi‑Fi as the server.
  • Confirm Apache/MySQL (or your stack) is running.
  • Cached screens may open, but reports and data need the server online.
